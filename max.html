Meet Max

Max is a vibrant and playful 3-year-old parrot with a love for mimicking sounds and chatting. He’s full of personality and has an impressive repertoire of words and phrases! Max loves being the center of attention and is sure to keep you entertained with his antics.

With his colorful feathers and bright, intelligent eyes, Max is not just a pet – he's a friend who loves interaction and has a huge, loving heart.

Care Requirements

  • Daily mental stimulation (Max loves toys and puzzles!)
  • A spacious cage for flying and playing
  • Regular social interaction (Max thrives on companionship!)
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables for a healthy diet
  • Time outside the cage for supervised flying and exploring

Fun Facts About Max

  • Max can mimic human speech and often greets people with a “Hello!”
  • He loves to dance to music, especially when it has a good beat!
  • Max is a bit of a foodie and loves to try new fruits – his favorite is mango!
