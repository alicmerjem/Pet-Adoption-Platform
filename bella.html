Meet Bella

Bella is a playful, energetic 2-year-old dog with a loving heart. She’s always up for a game of fetch, a run in the park, or just cuddling on the couch. Bella is a loyal companion who loves spending time with her human family and gets along great with other dogs.

With her beautiful coat and big brown eyes, Bella is as charming as she is friendly. She’s a natural at making new friends, whether it's people or other pets!
