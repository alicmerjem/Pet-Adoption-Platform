Frequently Asked Questions

PetLink is a platform for adopting pets in your local area. You can browse through the available animals and find your perfect companion.

To adopt a pet, simply register on the website, browse through the available animals, and submit an adoption application for the pet you wish to adopt.
To register, click on the 'Registration' link in the menu, fill in the necessary information, and submit your application. Once registered, you'll be able to browse available pets and apply for adoption.
You will need a valid government-issued ID, proof of address, and a completed adoption application form. Additional documents may be required depending on the animal you're adopting.
