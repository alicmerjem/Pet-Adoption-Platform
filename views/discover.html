Free Vet Visits

When you adopt a pet from PetLink, you’ll receive free visits to our partnered vet clinic! We want to ensure that your new furry friend stays healthy and happy. These visits cover initial health check-ups, vaccinations, and dietary advice to make the transition to their new home as smooth as possible.
