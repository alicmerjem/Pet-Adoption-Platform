Meet Luna

Luna is a sweet, gentle 2-year-old black and white rabbit with a calm demeanor and a curious nature. She loves hopping around and exploring her surroundings, especially when it comes to finding a cozy spot to relax. Luna is a friendly companion and enjoys being gently petted and given plenty of attention.

Her striking black and white fur makes her stand out, but it’s her sweet personality that truly shines. Luna is looking for a loving home where she can receive lots of cuddles and care!

Care Requirements

  • A spacious area to hop around and explore
  • Fresh hay, vegetables, and water daily
  • Gentle interaction and handling
  • Regular grooming to keep her coat healthy
  • Safe, quiet environment away from loud noises

Fun Facts About Luna

  • Luna loves to dig through soft bedding, making little burrows.
  • She has a favorite toy: a small stuffed carrot that she loves to nudge around.
  • Though she enjoys quiet moments, Luna is also very curious and loves exploring new places.
