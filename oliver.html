Meet Oliver

Oliver is an adorable 1-year-old hamster with a playful and curious personality. He loves to run on his wheel, explore tunnels, and nibble on his favorite treats. Although he's small in size, Oliver's big personality makes him a perfect companion for anyone looking for a pet with a lot of energy and charm!

Oliver has a soft, golden-brown coat and is always ready to entertain with his little adventures. He enjoys his quiet moments in his cozy bedding, but he's always up for a good play session when it's time to explore!
