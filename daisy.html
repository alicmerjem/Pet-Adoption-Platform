Meet Daisy

Daisy is a 2-year-old Portuguese Podengo with a charming and energetic personality. She loves to run and play, especially outdoors, and is always eager for her next adventure. Known for her boundless energy and keen instincts, Daisy is a perfect companion for active families or individuals who love to be outdoors!

With her short coat and elegant stance, Daisy is not just a beautiful dog but also an intelligent one. She’s quick to learn new tricks and enjoys staying mentally engaged with puzzles and games. Her playful nature and loyal heart make her an ideal pet for those who can match her enthusiasm and love for life!

Care Requirements

  • Daily exercise to match her energy level, including walks and runs
  • Interactive play sessions with toys or puzzles to keep her mentally stimulated
  • Regular grooming to keep her coat clean and healthy
  • Consistent training and positive reinforcement to help her learn new commands
  • A secure yard or outdoor space for her to run and play safely

Fun Facts About Daisy

  • Daisy loves to dig and will often create little “burrows” in the garden.
  • She’s an excellent swimmer and loves to cool off in the water on hot days!
  • Her keen nose makes her a natural at scent games, and she loves tracking scents around the yard.
